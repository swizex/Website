A public oath of my journalistic principles

I am a big believer of writing this on record, not just so the public will be aware of things, but also knowing that if i find myself in the situation of unknowingly going against my won principles i can be rest assured that the public will call me out on it, and i am grateful for this kind of correction mechanisms, because with no input from external sources, i will just end tangled up i an irony that i have set to destroy originally.

With that out of the way, i also need to address an important looming question, why do i write the things i write?

The answer would be:

I am writing down the things that intrigued me, be it artistic, abstract in nature, or scientific theories and research.

I am documenting my intellectual life on “paper” so others can be shared with my experience ,thinking and opinions.

I do not do this as a full time job nor a hobby, it is merely a written reflection of my own inner thinking,progressions and future workings.

You are more than welcome to dabble in my thinking and criticize it, i will consider all input i get, but i am not obliged to them, and will act on my best judgment at the time knowing i will be as aware as i can on other narratives and world views and adjust accordingly.

thank you for following my personal journey.
